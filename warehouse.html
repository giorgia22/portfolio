Warehouse

High-school final project: a small-scale warehouse shelving.

GitHub

Project description

In high-school me and my teammates decided to do an automatic warehouse to bring as our final project to our graduation exam. I followed both the electronic part and the programming part of the project.

The warehouse is controlled through a numpad with which you can choose to repose or retrieve a mini-pallet from the warehouse or change the position of a pallet inside the warehouse, after the command is sent there's a mobile platform that pulls up and places down the mini-pallets in the given warehouse cells or in the user location.
The whole project is powered by Arduino.

Role

PCBs design and programming.

Challenge:

I tried to control the stepper motors without any library.
