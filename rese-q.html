Rese.Q

PCBs realization for a prototype robot in a student team at PoliTO.

Project description

At university I'm a member of the student team ISAAC, whose application area is the search and rescue (SAR) robotics.
Rese.Q is our first prototype, it has a unique design, it's modular and has two working configuration: snake-like robot and arm robot. Since it's the first prototype of the team, it has been used to try and test our ideas.

In this project I'm group leader of the electronic part and I participate in the researches for components to use, in the design of the wiring diagram and PCBs and also in the soldering part for our stripboards.

Role

Research and design of PCBs.

Challenge:

For sure dealing with SMD components, since I haven't had any chance before with this type of components.

Skills

Design Research Soldering Altium
