Comparse Cercasi!

"Comparse Cercasi!" is an alternate reality game, created for the transmedia course.

Live Demo Trailer Youtube PowerPoint

Project description

An ARG is an experience in which the reality and the game join together in a way they can't be distinguished anymore.

This is what Comparse Cercasi! is, an experience to encourage people to interact with the statues of the Mufant museums in Turin, by believing that they have to use the statues to participate in a casting of the Fantastic Pictures production.
The users will discover at the end of the experience that the film company was composed by the fictional characters represented in the statues, that wanted to be remembered from the people, so they built this experience for them.

In this project I was in the storytelling group, so I wrote the texts used in the ARG (flyers, posts on Instagram and Faacebook, messages from the characters, ...) and I participated in the drafting of the story.
