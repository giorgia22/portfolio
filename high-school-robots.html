RoboCup Jr robots

All the robots I realized with my high-school robotics team.

Project description

I participated for 3 years in the Maze RoboCup Jr, arriving to the national championships.

The competition requires the realization of a robot that can navigate a maze, by also detecting areas to avoid, and identify "people" (hot resistors) that need a "first-aid" kit, that the robot has to leave.

I created the PCBs for all the robots from scratch. I followed the whole process from the design of the board, to the realization with the etching process and finally to the assembly and soldering part.

Role

Design, manufacturing and assembly.

Challenge:

This were my first PCBs so I had to do a lot of attempts before having everything right.

Skills

Design Manual abilities Multisim Ultiboard
