Arrietty 3D room

3D room created in Blender taking inspiration from the animated film "Arrietty".

Live Demo GitHub

Project description

At university I attended the Computer Graphic course and as final project we had to create a 3D room from scratch by using Blender for the modeling and the rendering and Adobe Substance for creating the materials.

Me and another student collaborated to recreate the living room of the animated film “Arrietty”.

Since our reference was an animated film, we chose a non-realistic style for the objects. The film is about a family of tiny people who secretly live in the walls of a house, borrowing items from humans, for this reason in our project there are some typical little objects (like buttons, jars, stamps, …) that are recreated bigger.

Role

Design, illumination and render.

Challenge:

Creating a realistic illumination of the scene, balancing too much or too little light, especially in the night environment.

Skills

Design Modelling Rendering Camera positioning Blender Adobe Substance
